Features

Everything you need for digging

Have you been in a record store without a listening station? ...or the needle was broken? ...or just want to hear what you're holding - right now?

🎧

Instant Playback

Hear the record within seconds via Apple Music with full album context and tracklist. Falls back to YouTube when a match isn't on Apple Music.

🔍

Smart Recognition

OCR, on-device AI, barcode scanning, and album cover matching — multiple strategies work together to identify your records against a 7.5M+ catalog.

🎶

All Remixes

Find every remix, repress, and variant of any track via Discogs. Tap to play any version through Apple Music or YouTube.

💲

Discogs Integration

Log in with your Discogs account to manage your collection and wantlist, browse release images, and see marketplace pricing in multiple currencies.

📷

Share & Save

Share cards to Instagram Stories, save tracks to a prepare list for later, view your scan history, and print vinyl crate labels via AirPrint.

🔗

Add to Apple Music

Auto-add every record you scan directly to your Apple Music library — build your collection as you dig.

Scan Modes

Two ways to identify records

Whether you're flipping through a crate or looking up a specific pressing — there's a mode for it.

Scan

Continuous real-time scanning. Point your camera at a label, barcode, or album cover and Label Mate identifies it automatically using multi-frame consensus.

Search Pro

Type a catalog number or artist name for instant results with live-as-you-type filtering by format, decade, and master release. Also supports barcode scanning.
