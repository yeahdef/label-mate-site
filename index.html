Features

Everything you need at the crate

Label Mate reads text from record labels using on-device OCR and matches against a catalog of 7.5 million releases.

🎧

Instant Playback

Hear the record within seconds via Apple Music. Falls back to YouTube previews when a match isn't on Apple Music.

🔍

7.5M+ Catalog

On-device SQLite index of catalog numbers covers most commercially released records. Network connection used for playback and additional metadata.

🎶

DJ Tools

Key detection with Camelot codes, BPM analysis, waveform visualization, pitch fader, looper, and tap BPM.

💲

Discogs Pricing

See marketplace pricing, community stats, and condition breakdowns. Multi-currency support included.

📷

Multiple Scan Modes

Auto, manual, batch, spine scanning, and more. There's a mode for every situation — from quick crate digs to cataloging collections.

🔗

Add to Apple Music

Auto-add every record you scan directly to your Apple Music library — build your collection as you dig.

Scan Modes

A mode for every situation

Whether you're flipping through a crate, typing catalog numbers, or scanning an entire shelf — Label Mate has you covered.

Auto

Continuous scanning with multi-frame consensus. Just point and play.

Manual

Freeze the frame and tap the text you want to search.

Matrix Pro

Type catalog or matrix numbers directly for offline lookup.

Focus

Guided multi-step scanning for tricky labels.

LP

Scan album covers — works even without readable text.

Batch Pro

Scan multiple records in a session. Great for cataloging.

Spine Pro

Scan record spines on a shelf without pulling them out.
